\hypertarget{intro}{%
\section{Intro}\label{intro}}

The Reichlab produces weekly forecasts of the percentage of doctors
visits that present with influenza-like-illness (ILI) at the state level
based on data provided through the CDC Flusight challenge. We use
historical ILI incidence to predict future ILI incidence using
statistical models.
